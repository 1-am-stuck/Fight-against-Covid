It is important to know if you the virus. Scroll down to know the common symptons

Most common symptoms:
1)fever
2)dry cough
3)tiredness

Less common symptoms:
1)aches and pains
2)sore throat
3)diarrhoea
4)conjunctivitis
5)headache
6)loss of taste or smell
7)a rash on skin, or discolouration of fingers or toes

Serious symptoms:
1)difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
2)chest pain or pressure
3)loss of speech or movement


It is important to note that most people who get covid don't always need medication. It you are experiencing any of the serious symptoms go to a hospital immediatly.
